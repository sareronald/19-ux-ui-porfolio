Sarah Ronald

I am an Australian UX/UI designer and front-end developer based in regional Armidale in northern NSW.

About Me

I am curious about people, systems and design. I am interested in designing delightful, human-centered experiences that shape how we interact with technology.

As a designer I aspire to solve problems with empathy and an inquiring mind. With each project my aim is to meet the need of the end-user, solving user problems through research, ideation, prototyping, testing and iteration provides endless opportunity to experiment and refine my skills.

Collaborating and communicating with others to build applications that are interactive, aesthetic, and easy to use is my passion. Excited about bringing my design knowledge, technical skills and enthusiasm to an organisation where collaboration and continuous learning is encouraged in a diverse and proactive team.
