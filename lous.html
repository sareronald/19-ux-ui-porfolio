Lou's Place

Redesign of a nonprofit organisation website.

Lou's Place is Sydney's only daytime refuge for women in need.

Project Overview

Lou's Place is a safe place for women to have their basic needs met. It is a community-based refuge for women in crisis, feeling isolated or needing support. Lou's provides the space for women to heal and receive the support they need to empower women to rebuild their lives.

Ideation

    Key Features

  • Quick and easy volunteer form that allows users to fill in skills & availability.
  • Display recent donation funded projects on the homepage so people know where their donations are going.
  • User can elect how their donated money is spent so that they have control over how the non-for-profit uses their donation.

Wireframes & Prototype

Testing and Iteration

    Tasks for User Testing

  • Task 1: Find the most recent donation funded project.
  • Task 2: Donate to the funding of a new dishwasher for the Redfern kitchen.
  • Task 3: You are a web developer looking to volunteer your time for a one off project at Lous place. You want to find out if you can use your professional skills to volunteer and if so, you're keen to register your interest.

    Feedback from User Testing

  • Confusion about downloading and uploading the contract? and entering WWCC number?
  • Confusion about Get Invloved tab and Volunteer button - how are they different?
  • page heirarchy: users missed the tabs at the top of the page (b/c they were attracted to the page title instead)
  • Users missed the second column of checkboxes in the volunteer section, thinking that they were a continuation of the left section.

Reflection

    What I learned

  • Variants are an excellent way of simplifying interactions in your design in Figma
  • Consistency is key when creating an effective user interface design
  • Ease of use is key to meeting the user's need(s)
  • As usual, I learnt alot from working with others

    Future Opportunities

  • Have a conversation with the Stakeholder before developing further - seek to understand the role of the website vs the role of their social media pages, where most of their updates are posted
  • Build out the rest of the pages
  • Volunteers Portal for registered volunteers could also be developed
  • Make the News page more prominent
